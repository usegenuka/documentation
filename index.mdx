# Why choose Genuka ?

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Built for Africa">
    Genuka is designed with the African business ecosystem in mind. From local
    currencies to regional payment methods, the platform adapts to the realities
    of African entrepreneurs, making it easier to sell, receive payments, and
    grow across borders.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Developer-Friendly Integration">
    Our APIs and SDKs are simple, secure, and well-documented, enabling
    developers to integrate Genuka seamlessly into any application. With clear
    guides, sandbox environments, and reliable support, you can focus on
    building while we handle the complexity of commerce.
  </Card>
</Columns>
